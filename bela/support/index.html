When reporting a problem

To help me fix it quickly, please include:

  • Your device model and iOS version
  • The app version (Settings → scroll to the bottom)
  • What you did and what happened vs. what you expected

Common questions

The card scanner isn't recognizing cards. The scanner is trained on Hungarian-suited cards and works best with good lighting and the cards laid flat. You can always enter points by hand instead.

My games aren't syncing. iCloud sync is a Pro feature — make sure you're signed into iCloud and that sync is enabled on each device.

I bought Pro but it's not unlocked. Open the paywall and tap "Restore purchase," or email me and I'll sort it out.
